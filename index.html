Who we are

We are Informatics.

We are a the Informatics Undergraduate Association (IUGA), and Women in Informatics (WINFO), Registered Student Organizations at the University of Washington. We are a team of passionate developers, designers, data scientists, program managers, consultants, and analysts with a fire to increase representation in STEM and share our amazing Informatics program to local high schools.

To learn more about the University of Washington's Informatics program, go to iSchool webpage.
